Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement explains how Decoding-Us.com (the "Website") handles the limited data processed during your interaction with our application, including the use of data from a federated platform.

1. Introduction

This statement outlines our practices regarding the processing of personal data in connection with Decoding-Us.com. As a Hypermedia as the Engine of Application State (HATEOAS) application focused on providing public data, we also integrate anonymized and public data from a federated platform to enhance our content.

2. Data We Collect

Currently, this Website does not actively collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or account information from our users. Our design does not require or facilitate user registration or login.

However, our server logs automatically record certain information when you access the Website, including:

  • Your IP address
  • The date and time of your request
  • The type of browser and operating system you are using (user-agent string)
  • The specific endpoints (URLs) you access

3. Use of Data from Federated Platform

To enhance the accuracy and detail of our haplogroup trees, we integrate anonymized and/or public data contributed by researchers through a federated platform for genetic genealogy. This incoming data may include genetic markers, haplogroups, and potentially aggregated geographic origins, processed in a way that aims to prevent individual re-identification.

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information from the users of this federated platform through this integration. The purpose of using this data is solely to improve the quality and scope of the information presented on Decoding-Us.com for the benefit of our users.

4. Purpose of Our Data Processing

The information collected in our server logs is used for the following limited purposes:

  • Ensuring the proper functioning and security of the Website.
  • Basic analysis of website traffic to understand usage patterns and improve performance.
  • Diagnosing technical issues and preventing malicious activity.

This data is generally not used to identify individual users in a personally identifiable way.

5. Data Retention

Server logs are typically retained by our hosting provider or according to our server configuration for a period of 30 days. After this period, the logs are usually deleted or anonymized.

6. Data Sharing

Currently, Decoding-Us.com does not share any personal data of our users with the federated platform as part of this integration. The data flow is primarily incoming to enhance our content.

7. Your Rights and International Data Protection Laws

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) in California, and similar regulations elsewhere, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These rights can include the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and object to the processing of your personal data, as well as the right to data portability.

While the scope of personal data we currently process from our users is limited to server logs, we are committed to respecting these rights where applicable. If you have any inquiries regarding your rights under relevant data protection laws and how they might apply to the limited data we process, please contact us using the information below.

8. Updates to this Privacy Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements, including the integration with the federated platform. We will post any changes on this page, and we encourage you to review this statement periodically.
